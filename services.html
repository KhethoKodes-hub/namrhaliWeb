Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics services

What areas do you serve?

We provide logistics services across South Africa with specialized expertise in the Mpumalanga region. Our national network allows us to serve clients throughout the country efficiently.

How do you ensure on-time delivery?

We utilize advanced route planning technology, real-time tracking, and maintain buffer capacity to handle unexpected delays. Our 24/7 operations center monitors all shipments to ensure they stay on schedule.

What is your B-BBEE status?

We are proud to be a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor with 135% procurement recognition. Our certification is valid until June 2026.

Do you offer temperature-controlled transport?

Yes, we have a fleet of temperature-controlled vehicles and specialized warehousing facilities to handle perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive products.

How can I track my shipment?

We provide real-time tracking through our customer portal. Once your shipment is booked, you'll receive login credentials to monitor your goods throughout the delivery process.
