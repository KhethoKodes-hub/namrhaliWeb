From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2022 in Middelburg, Mpumalanga, Namrhali Projects began with a simple vision: to provide reliable, efficient logistics solutions that empower South African businesses to thrive. What started as a small local operation has rapidly grown into a trusted partner for companies across the nation.

Our founder, recognizing the critical role of logistics in economic development, established Namrhali Projects with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and community empowerment. This foundation has guided our growth and continues to shape our operations today.

As a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor, we're proud to be part of South Africa's economic transformation journey while maintaining the highest standards of service delivery and customer satisfaction.

Our Commitment to Transformation

As a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor, Namrhali Projects is deeply committed to South Africa's economic transformation. Our certification reflects our dedication to:

  • Creating meaningful employment opportunities
  • Developing skills within our workforce
  • Supporting black-owned suppliers and partners
  • Contributing to socio-economic development
  • Promoting enterprise development initiatives

Our B-BBEE status isn't just a certificate—it's a reflection of our core values and our commitment to building a more inclusive South African economy.
